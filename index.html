Questions

Frequently asked

What areas do you serve?
We serve Akron, Cuyahoga Falls, Stow, Hudson, Tallmadge, and Barberton in Summit County, Ohio. Just outside these? Ask — we may still be able to help.
Do you handle same-day Airbnb turnovers?
Yes. We schedule turnovers around your checkout and check-in times and offer same-day service for tight, back-to-back bookings (subject to availability). Same-day/emergency turns carry a 25% rush rate.
Do you provide fresh linens and restocking?
Yes. We rotate fresh linen sets, launder as needed, and restock essentials such as toilet paper, paper towels, coffee, and toiletries from a checklist you set up with us.
How does your pricing work?
We price flat-rate by the job, not by the hour, so you always know the cost up front. Airbnb turnovers start at $115 and recurring home cleans start at $159, with discounts of up to 20% for weekly and biweekly service. See the full pricing page.
Will I get proof the clean was done?
Always. We send before-and-after photos with every turnover so you know your space is guest-ready before anyone arrives — and we flag any damage or low supplies we spot.
How do I book?
Request a free quote through our contact form or email hello@linenkey.com. We typically reply the same day with a flat-rate quote.

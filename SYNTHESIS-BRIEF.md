## 1. Identity
- **Business name:** Linen & Key
- **Domain:** linenkey.com *(no live cleaning company found using the name; confirm exact registrar availability at checkout)*
- **Tagline (selected):** **Guest-ready turnovers. Home-ready clean.**
  *Chosen because it is the only option that explicitly serves both audiences (STR hosts AND homeowners) and its parallel rhythm reads premium.*
- **Brand personality:** Linen & Key brings boutique-hotel polish to everyday homes and short-term rentals across Summit County. Warm, precise, and quietly luxurious — the kind of clean you feel the moment you walk in, whether you live there or just checked in. Reliable as a front desk.
- **Service area:** Akron, Cuyahoga Falls, Stow, Hudson, Tallmadge, Barberton (Summit County, OH)

## 4. Pricing (researched — real Summit County market, "premium but competitive")
**Airbnb / STR turnover (flat-rate, starting at):** 1BR/Studio **$115** · 2BR **$155** · 3BR **$215** · 4BR **$295**. Same-day/emergency +25%.

**Residential standard, one-time (starting at, by size):** Under 1,500 sqft **$159** · 1,500–2,500 **$219** · 2,500–3,500 **$299** · 3,500+ **$389**.

**Recurring discount off standard:** Weekly **−20%** · Biweekly **−15%** · Monthly **−10%**.

**Deep clean:** from **$325** (scales to $475+ on larger/heavier homes).
**Move-in / move-out:** from **$275** (scales to $500+).

**Add-ons:** Inside fridge **$35** · Inside oven **$40** · Interior windows **$7/window** · Inside cabinets (empty) **$40** · Linen rotation & laundry **$30/load** · Pet surcharge **$20**.

*Market note:* Akron is price-sensitive; competitors advertise hourly ($21–24/hr). Linen & Key prices **flat-rate by job** to read premium and protect margin. STR turnover specialists are thin locally — that's the wedge.

## 5. STR market context
~650–800+ active short-term rentals across Akron + Cuyahoga Falls + suburbs (estimated). Akron ≈61% occupancy / ≈$131 ADR; Cuyahoga Falls ≈63% / ≈$164 ADR. Demand driven by Cuyahoga Valley National Park, Blossom Music Center, university/medical visitors.

## 6. Copy hooks
**Airbnb host (lead with these — they're the #1 complaints):**
1. **Never miss a turnover.** No-shows and last-minute cancellations are hosts' single biggest cleaning complaint.
2. **Photo proof every turn.** Before-and-after photos sent before guests arrive — no driving over to check.
3. **Protect your 4.8+ and Superhost status.** One review mentioning hair, dust, or odor drags your rating; we make sure it never does.
4. **Same-day & tight turnarounds** built around your checkout/check-in, with **calendar sync**.
5. **Fresh linen rotation + restocking** from your checklist (toiletries, paper, coffee).
6. **A 100-point checklist runs between every stay.**

**Homeowner:**
1. The same trusted pro every visit — **consistency you can count on**.
2. **Come home to something right** — weekly, biweekly, or monthly.
3. Deep cleans and move-in/move-out done right the first time.

**Host vocabulary to echo verbatim:** turnover, back-to-back bookings, tight turnaround, guest-ready, same-day clean, no-shows, calendar sync, photo proof, restocking essentials, fresh linens, checklist every stay, 5-star clean, protect my reviews.
